# Supply-Chain-Dashboard
A comprehensive Supply Chain Analytics solution built in Power BI.
It simulates how a global company like Samsung can monitor and optimize its operations across:
1. Suppliers
2. Inventory & Production
3. Shipment & Logistics
4. Customer Delivery
The dashboard transforms raw operational data into actionable insights for business decision-making.

## Objective: The goal of this dashboard is to answer key business questions:
Which suppliers are most efficient and reliable?
Is inventory aligned with demand and safety levels?
Where are shipment delays happening and why?
Which customers and channels drive the most revenue?
